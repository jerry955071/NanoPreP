%(meanq)

Parameter Optimization

Adapter/primer (AP) sequences of different lengths are aligned to each read two times.

  • The first alignment is treated as the real one (blue).
  • The second alignment is treated as a random one (orange).

NanoPrePro then finds the best combination of AP length, similarity cutoff, and location cutoffs to tell real alignments apart from random ones.
